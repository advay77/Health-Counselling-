NEET UG Company & Counselling Support

We expertly handle NEET UG counselling for both All India Quota (AIQ) and state-level seats:

  • All India (MCC) Counselling: Guidance for 15% AIQ, Deemed Universities, Central Universities, and ESIC/AFMC seats.
  • State Counselling: Personalized support for state quota seats in states like Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, and more.
  • Choice Filling & Locking: Strategic advice to maximize your chances based on your NEET score and preferences.
  • Document Verification: Step-by-step help with all required documents for both national and state processes.
  • Updates & Alerts: Timely notifications about important dates, cutoffs, and seat allotment results.

Our experienced team ensures you never miss a deadline and get the best possible seat as per your eligibility and aspirations.
